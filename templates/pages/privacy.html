Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data E LINK collects, why, and how it's protected.

On this page Your consent Data security Payment data Marketing Third-party sharing Storage & retention Cookies

01 Your consent

When you create an account or place an order (with or without an account), we ask you to confirm you've read and agree to this Privacy Policy before we store or use your personal data. The data we collect is limited to what's needed to process your purchase and delivery: name, email, phone, and delivery address.

02 How we protect your data

Our site is served over HTTPS, and login/session cookies are marked secure so credentials and personal data are encrypted in transit. We do not store card numbers or CVV codes on our servers — see the Payment data section below.

03 Payment data

Currently, the only available payment method is Cash on Delivery, so no card or bank details are collected at checkout. When we enable online card payments, they will be processed through a PCI DSS–compliant payment gateway — your card details will be sent directly to that gateway and will never be stored on E LINK's servers.

04 Marketing materials

We do not currently share your contact information with any partner or third party for marketing or promotional purposes. If this changes, we'll update this section to name who we share data with and how it's used, and we'll ask for your separate consent before sending marketing communications.

05 Third-party data sharing

Your personal information is not sold, rented, or leased to any third party. We only share the minimum necessary data with service providers who help us operate the store — for example, delivery couriers (to fulfill your order) and our database/hosting provider (to store it securely) — and with government or law enforcement authorities where required by Qatari law. Some pages load fonts from Google Fonts, which receives your browser's IP address when the page loads; this is limited to serving font files and isn't linked to your E LINK account.

06 Data collection & storage

Account and order data (name, contact details, delivery address, order history) is stored in our database, hosted with Contabo in the European Union (Germany). We keep this data for as long as your account is active, and order records for as long as required by Qatari tax and commercial law after that; once data is no longer required for these purposes, it is deleted. You can request deletion of your account data at any time by emailing support@elinkonline.com, subject to records we're legally required to keep.

07 Cookies

We use a small number of cookies, all necessary for the site to function:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in and remembers your cart between pages.
  • CSRF cookie — protects forms (login, checkout) from cross-site request forgery.

We don't currently use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies. If that changes, this section will be updated and, where required, we'll ask for your consent first.
