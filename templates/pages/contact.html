Complaints procedure

If something about your order, delivery, or an item you purchased isn't right, we want to hear about it and put it right.

  1. Email support@elinkonline.com with your order number and a description of the issue (photos help for damaged or incorrect items).
  2. We acknowledge every complaint within 2 business days.
  3. We aim to resolve most complaints — replacement, refund, or exchange — within 7 business days of acknowledgment. See our returns, damages, and warranty terms for details.
  4. If you're not satisfied with the outcome, ask to have your complaint escalated to a manager for review.
